About Us

Breadcrumbs was founded in Bridgewater, VA, in October 2022, and every day we have strived for excellence in our product and ourselves. We saw the need for a personal alert system in our own lives, and wanted to share that vision with the world. Everyone deserves to feel safe, especially when doing activities on our own. In the case of an emergency, Breadcrumbs hopes to point your loved ones in the right direction, using your message as the beginning of the trail. Let us help you prepare for the worst.

Our Mission Statement

Breadcrumbs' mission is to provide a safety net for individuals who find themselves tackling life out on their own and improve their peace of mind. We do this through our asynchronous message service, which allows users to send a quick message with valuable information to a friend, parent, or partner after a given time to alert them of a need for assistance.
